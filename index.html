How to Play

  • Modes: Play Classic Genre, Artist-Specific, or Guess the Movie!
  • Today Ten: A daily synced challenge where everyone in the world plays the exact same 10 songs.
  • Custom Filters: Separate keywords by commas (e.g. 1980s, Pop, Madonna) to build ultra-specific challenges.
  • Epic Intros (Hard): Set the level to Hard to only get a 10-second snippet of the song!
  • 750 Point Ceiling: Scores are mathematically normalized to a perfect 750, no matter how many rounds you play.
