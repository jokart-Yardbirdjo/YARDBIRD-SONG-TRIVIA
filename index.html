How to Play & Scoring

  • The Goal: Listen to the song and click "STOP & GUESS" before the timer runs out.
  • Speed Points: Your base score is 30 - Seconds Elapsed. The faster you stop the clock, the more points you earn!
  • Perfect Double: Guess BOTH the Artist and the Song Title correctly to multiply your round score by 2x.
  • Close Enough: Don't worry about perfect spelling! If your guess matches 60% of the significant words in the title, you get credit.
  • Streak Bonus: Get 3 rounds correct in a row to earn an instant +50 Point Bonus! (Failing both fields resets your streak).
  • Trivia Hints: If enabled, a hint showing the release year, initials, and a fun fact will appear halfway through the timer.
