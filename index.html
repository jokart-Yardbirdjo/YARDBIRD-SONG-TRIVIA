How to Play

  • Modes: Play Classic Genre, Artist-Specific, or Guess the Movie!
  • Today Three: A daily synced 3-round challenge spanning the 80s, 90s, and 2000s.
  • The Lifeline (Easy/Medium): If you don't guess before the timer hits 10 seconds, Multiple Choice options will automatically appear to save you.
  • Epic Intros (Hard): Set the level to Hard to only get a 10-second snippet (No lifeline!).
  • Scoring: Scores are driven by the timer. The faster you guess, the more points you get. If you wait for the Lifeline, your max points are capped at 10. Normalizes to 750 max!
