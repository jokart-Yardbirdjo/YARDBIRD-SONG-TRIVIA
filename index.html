How to Play

  • Modes: Play Classic Genre, Artist-Specific, or Guess the Movie!
  • Formats: Choose between Multiple Choice or Classic Typing.
  • Multiple Choice: Options appear instantly! Click the right answer before time runs out.
  • Typing Mode: Hit "STOP" to pause the song. You'll get a 20s Hot Seat timer to type your guess.
  • Close Enough: Misspellings are fine if you are typing. A few correct words will trigger a match!
